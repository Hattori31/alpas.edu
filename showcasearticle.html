AT THE M4 WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS, ECHO BROKEN THE CODE OF BLACKLIST INTERNATIONAL

Written By Dionne Urbina January 22, 2023
Sports

MANILA, Philippines — ECHO broke the nearly impenetrable code and triumphantly took home the title of best Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Team in the world on Jan. 15 in Jakarta, Indonesia. The Orcas dominated the M4 Championship Grand Finals with a 4-0 sweep in a best-of-seven series and decimated Blacklist International's tried-and-true strategy. Millions of Filipino fans tuned in to watch the epic battle between the two teams thanks to Smart, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang's long-time official telco partner in the Philippines.

It was a rematch of the M4 World Championship upper bracket finals as well as the MPL Philippines Season 10 final, when Blacklist International successfully defended its title and solidified its dynasty. Unexpectedly, the agents were unable to directly respond to how they would prevent ECHO from deciphering their game plan—the Ultimate Bonding Experience (UBE) strategy—which was a surprising development. What rules did ECHO break in order to defeat Blacklist International's uncompromising resolve and win a decisive match?

These five elements made up the ECHO Express Strategy, which helped them win:

1. Pay attention to your rival.

2. Organize the crowd.

3. Choose the proper targets.

4. Be adaptable.

5. You receive the MVP with patience.

"Serbisyong pangkalayaan para sa buong bayan"
