About Our Organization

We would like to introduce to you Alpas, a Filipino media company that we consider to be one of the top news networks. "Alpas," which means "to become free" or "to burst loose" in the Filipino language, refers to a non-profit, non-governmental youth organization that works to de-stigmatize and liberate young people from harmful cultures. Alpas is a modern, trustworthy news source in our nation that disseminates timely, accurate information while focusing on the propagation of false Information.
